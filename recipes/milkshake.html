Chocolate Milkshake

Cool, creamy, and irresistibly chocolatey – a homemade chocolate milkshake is the perfect treat on a warm day or as a dessert any time. With just a few simple ingredients, you can blend together a thick, rich shake that beats any drive‑thru version. Top it with whipped cream and extra chocolate syrup for a truly EPIC experience!

    Ingredients:
  • 2 cups chocolate ice cream
  • 1/2 cup whole milk (or any milk you prefer)
  • 2 tablespoons chocolate syrup, plus more for drizzling
  • Whipped cream (optional, for topping)
  • Cherry (optional)
    Steps:
  1. Add the chocolate ice cream, milk, and chocolate syrup to a blender.
  2. Blend on medium speed until smooth and creamy. If the shake is too thick, add a little more milk; if it's too thin, add a bit more ice cream.
  3. Drizzle chocolate syrup around the inside of a tall glass for a decorative look (optional).
  4. Pour the milkshake into the glass.
  5. Top with a generous dollop of whipped cream, a drizzle of chocolate syrup, and a cherry if desired.
  6. Serve immediately in a cool glass so you can feel the cold trough your fingers!
  7. Drink
