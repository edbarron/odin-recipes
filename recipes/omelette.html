Omelette

The most delicious way to prepare your eggs in the morning! Nothing adds protein to an early breakfast like a good old golden omelette. It's very easy to make, and with ordinary ingredients like cheese and ham it can turn into a morning masterpiece. There's a reason why professional athletes love omelettes, you know!

    Ingredients:
  • 2 Eggs or more
  • Butter
  • cheese
  • Ham
  • Salt
    Steps:
  1. Break your eggs into a cup, a plate or something similar to contain them and use a fork to mix them until you have a viscous, yellow liquid. Sprinkle some salt on it if you want to.
  2. Use the butter to prepare the pan, and when it's hot enough, just pour your eggs on it and let them cook.
  3. While the omelette is cooking, prepare your ham and cheese!
  4. Wait until you see the "liquid eggs" have become more solid and "fluffy". Now that the eggs are solid enough and look like a tortilla, it's time to flip it, so the other side gets cooked as well.
  5. Now lay your ham, cheese, and any other ingredient that you added on it and wrap it.
  6. Optional: add bacon, veggies, mushrooms or whatever you like.
  7. Eat.
