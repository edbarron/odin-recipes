Burgers

This is a classic of all times... the hamburger is like a sandwich on steroids, the bread is bigger, the meat is juicier, and if you throw in some bacon and cheese then it really is all you need in life. Don't forget to include your veggies!

    Ingredients:
  • Burger buns
  • Ground meat 80/20
  • American cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Butter
    Steps:
  1. Spread butter on the inner side of your bun, then just let it cook in the pan
  2. Take the ground meat and make a ball the size of your hand. Then, in a second pan, smash the meatball so it spreads in a circle
  3. Prepare your veggies! Disinfect them and cut them into slices! Get the tomato, the onion and the lettuce ready!
  4. Wait for your bun to have that golden toasted look from the butter, and the meat to look darker while it cooks
  5. Assemble! Use your bun as the lower floor... then add the meat, a slice of American cheese, then the lettuce, the onion, the tomato and the other side of the bun on top.
  6. Optional: add bacon, mayo, mustard, ketchup, chicken or whatever your imagination takes you
  7. Eat
